FAQ

💬 Do I need to be fit to join HerFit?

Not at all! HerFit welcomes women of all fitness levels — whether you’re a beginner or experienced. Our trainers will help you find the perfect starting point.

💬 What should I bring to my first class?

Just bring comfortable workout clothes, a water bottle, and a positive attitude! We provide all the equipment you’ll need.

💬 Do you offer beginner-friendly classes?

Absolutely! Many of our yoga, dance fitness, and strength classes are beginner-friendly. Just let your instructor know you’re new, and they’ll guide you!

You can easily book through our website, call us directly, or visit the front desk at the gym. We recommend booking early to reserve your spot!

💬 Are your classes for women only?

Yes! HerFit is a women-only space, designed to create a supportive and empowering environment for all women.
